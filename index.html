Dentistry for Today's Family

Dentistry has changed a lot since you were a kid. However, the power of a beautiful, healthy smile remains constant. Dr. Christopher Madsen’s mission is to use the most advanced dental technology to help you enjoy a lifetime of healthy smiles.

A graduate of the prestigious University of Washington School of Dentistry, Dr. Madsen has acquired extensive post-doctoral study in cosmetic dentistry, laser dentistry, decay prevention and restoration. This experience in advanced dentistry gives him a valuable perspective into the best ways to maintain and restore your family’s smiles.
